Air Quality Around the World!

About

Let's say you’re a wealthy college student and you’re trying to figure out where to study abroad next semester! You don’t want to shave too many years off of your life during these 4 months so you want to see what the air quality is like in select cities around the world. Look no further! Our website allows you to see the amount of particulates per square volume of 7 different harmful air pollutants. Just select your city and select your pollutant and WALLAH, you got the information you need.
